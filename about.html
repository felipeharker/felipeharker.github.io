Biography

Felipe Graduated from the University of Houston Hines College of Architecture and Design in Spring of 2024 with a Bachelor of Architecture.
While at University of Houston, Felipe developed a passion for photography, and joined the McGovern College of the Arts as a marketing intern for many years. He was the editor of an architecture and documentary photography page at U of H called UH: Close Up.
Since graduating, Felipe has worked in commercial signage and architectural products, overseeing the design, fabrication, and installation of numerous projects in Texas and elsewhere. Beyond that, Felipe has led the effort to incorporate parametric and computational design practices at his company.
In his free time, Felipe enjoys running, college football (Go Coogs!), and playing New York Times games.
